About Me

Hi, I’m Logan Gould. Originally from Dunfermline, Scotland, I now live on the sunny west coast of California. I’m a truck mechanic working for Hoydon, and I have a passion for working on classic cars and driving my Nissan Z. I love car movies, and when I'm not in the garage, you can find me travelling, especially in Japan!

Work

Truck Mechanic

As a truck mechanic at Hoydon, I work on a variety of vehicles, ensuring they’re running in top condition. My background allows me to tackle complex mechanical challenges with precision and care.

Mechanical Engineering

With a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, I have the skills and knowledge to solve mechanical problems that require a deep understanding of engineering principles.

Classic Car Restorations

My passion for working on classic cars has allowed me to restore and maintain some incredible vintage vehicles, preserving their beauty and functionality.
